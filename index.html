About East African Girls in Tech

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to bridging the gender gap in technology across East Africa.

Our Mission

East African Girls in Tech (EAGIT) was founded in 2020 with the mission to empower young women across East Africa with technology skills that can transform their lives and communities.

We provide free and affordable training in various tech fields, mentorship programs, and career placement assistance to help our graduates secure meaningful employment in the tech industry.

Our headquarters is located in Johannesburg, South Africa, with training centers across East Africa serving girls from all backgrounds.

Software Development

Course Overview

Our comprehensive Software Development course equips you with the skills needed to become a full-stack developer. You'll learn both front-end and back-end technologies to build modern web applications.

Course Duration

6 months (24 weeks) of intensive training

Part-time options available (extends to 9 months)

What You'll Learn

  • HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript (ES6+) fundamentals
  • Front-end development with React.js
  • Back-end development with Node.js and Express
  • Database design with MongoDB and SQL
  • RESTful API development
  • Version control with Git and GitHub
  • Deployment and DevOps basics
  • Agile development methodologies

Certification

Upon completion, you'll receive our Software Development Certificate. We also prepare you for relevant industry certifications (free exam voucher for girls).

Career Opportunities

  • Front-end Developer
  • Back-end Developer
  • Full-stack Developer
  • Web Application Developer
  • Software Engineer
